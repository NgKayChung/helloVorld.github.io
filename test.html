About Us


Vault-Tec is an Award winning 5 star leisure and business hotel in Kuala Lumpur that will allow you to relax and unwind.


Our ethos of 5 star service in a 4 star environment at 3 star prices will ensure your visit to Kuala Lumpur will be a pleasant experience. Whether you are travelling to Putrajaya on Business, Shopping in Kuala Lumpur, need a hotel near The Twin Towers or the SECC for a conference or exhibition, attending a concert at the city or just having a romantic break in Kuala Lumpur, we look forward to welcoming you! And we offer free on site parking and free Wi-fi throughout the hotel.

Experience a haven of luxury and style


Expect a very flexible approach to your room requirements. Fluffy towels and extra bedding as required. We also offer room service with no additional tray charges. Family rooms complemented by childrens’ menus although most of our discerning children prefer the adult menus! And an on site laundry service.


All with every modern facility, as required by the discerning guest, including direct dial telephone, hairdryer, iron and ironing board (on request) Flat screen TV and FREE Wi-Fi. Each room decorated using the high quality fabrics and furnishings to ensure your comfort and relaxation
